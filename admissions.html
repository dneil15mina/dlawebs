Admissions

Application for Entrance Test

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

  • Nursery - 3 years to 3 years & 11 mos.
  • Pre-Kinder - 4 years to 4 years & 11 mos.
  • Kinder - 5 years to 5 years & 11 mos.
  • Grade I - 6 years to 6 years & 11 mos.
  • Grade II to High School – as per DepEd Requirements
  • APPLICATION FORM *
  • Certified True Copy of Report Card (No grade lower than 83%)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character and Discipline from Guidance Counselor/Coordinator or School Head/Principal
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate *
  • Two 1x1 I.D. pictures taken recently *
  • Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate *
  • Medical Certificate from any competent doctor *
  • RECOMMENDATION FORM
  • Immunization Record (For Nursery to Gr 1 only) *
  • NCAE Test Result (For Senior High School)
  • DepEd Study Permit (For foreign applicants only)
  • Long Brown Envelope (To be submitted with the requirements) *
Note * Requirements needed for Nursery Applicants

PROCEDURE FOR ADMISSION

Preschool and Grade School

  1. Submit the requirements at the Guidance Office to secure an application form.
  2. Fill up the application form.
  3. Pay the testing fee.
  4. The test is scheduled by the Guidance Personnel upon presentation of testing fee Official Receipt. The applicant will be given an Entrance Examination Permit to be presented on the day of the test. The date of release of the test results will be indicated on the said permit.
  5. If all the required documents are submitted, test results will be released on the day itself for Preschool applicants and after (3) three working days for Grade School applicants. (No test results will be released over the phone)

High School follow steps 1 – 4.

  1. The applicant will be notified of the result of the written test.
  2. Applicants who passed the written test are scheduled for an interview with the Guidance Coordinator and physical inspection by the School Nurse/Physician follows. The final results are then released upon completion of all requirements.

VENUE

  • Divine Light Academy, LP – Manuela Subdivision., Las Piñas City
  • Divine Light Academy, Bacoor – Town & Country West Subd., Molino 3, Bacoor City, Cavite
  1. For new students, present test result, enrolment slip, probation contract & waiver form (for non-Catholics only) to the Registrar. For old students, present Original Report Card to the Registrar and secure enrolment form.
  2. Fill up enrolment form and present to the Registrar for checking.
  3. Proceed to the assessment section for the assessment of fees.
  4. Pay assessed fees to the cashier.
  5. Present Official Receipt and the Registrar’s copy to the Registrar and secure Admission Slip.
  • Annual payment – corresponding rebates are given.
  • Semi-annual payment – corresponding rebates are given.
